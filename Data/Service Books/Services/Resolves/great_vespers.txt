Based on the **Dolnytsky Typikon** (Parts 1â€“5), here is the fully unabridged structural outline for **Great Vespers (Vechirnya velyka)**.

This outline is the "Master Blueprint" for Saturday evenings, Great Feasts, and Vigils. It contains the logic gates necessary to resolve the variables from the Octoechos, Menaion, and Triodion.

### **Service Selector Logic**

* **Trigger Condition A (Sunday Vigil):** Saturday Evening.
* **Trigger Condition B (Festal Vigil):** Eve of a Rank 3 (Polyeleos), Rank 4 (Vigil), or Rank 5 (Great Feast) celebration.
* **Trigger Condition C (Lenten Sunday):** Sunday Evenings of Great Lent (often served with specific Prokeimena/Entrance).

---

### **I. The Enarxis (Opening)**

* **The Blessing:**
* **Resolve Case A (Vigil):** If connected to Matins (All-Night Vigil), "Glory to the Holy, Consubstantial...".
* **Resolve Case B (Separate):** If served separately, "Blessed is our God..."


* **The Usual Beginning:**
* "O Come, let us worship..." (Omitted if Vigil preceded by Great Compline/Litya).


* **The Proemial Psalm (Psalm 103):**
* **Rubric:** "Bless the Lord, O my soul."
* **Resolve Case A (Vigil):** Sung with slow, solemn melody (verses selected).
* **Resolve Case B (Simple Great Vespers):** Read (Recited) or sung simply.


* **The Great Litany:** "In peace let us pray to the Lord...".

---

### **II. The Kathisma (The Psalter)**

* **The Logic Gate (Part 1 & 2):**
* **Resolve Case A (Saturday Evening):** **1st Kathisma** ("Blessed is the man") is sung in full or abbreviated.
* **Resolve Case B (Sunday Evening - Lenten):** No Kathisma (unless specified).
* **Resolve Case C (Feast Eve falling on Sun/Mon):** **1st Kathisma** is sung.
* **Resolve Case D (Feast Eve on Tue-Fri):** Usually **No Kathisma**, unless the Typikon explicitly prescribes the Kathisma of the day (rare in parish practice, but Dolnytsky notes specific monastic rules).



---

### **III. "Lord, I Have Cried" (The Stichera)**

* **The Psalms (140, 141, 129, 116):** Sung in the **Tone of the Week** (if Sunday) or **Tone of the Feast**.
* **The Stichera "On Count" Logic (Part 1):**
* **Saturday Evening:** **10 Stichera** (Typically: 7 Resurrectional + 3 Saint OR 6 Res + 4 Saint).
* **Great Feast:** **8 or 10 Stichera** (All Festal).
* **Pre-Feast/Post-Feast:** Mixed Logic (e.g., 6 Feast + 4 Saint).


* **The "Glory" Slot:**
* **Resolve:** Insert **Doxastikon of the Saint** (Menaion) if applicable.


* **The "Both Now" Slot (The Dogmatikon):**
* **Resolve Case A (Saturday Evening):** Insert **Dogmatikon of the Tone** (Octoechos).
* **Resolve Case B (Feast):** Insert **Festal Theotokion** (Menaion).



---

### **IV. The Entrance & Readings**

* **The Entrance:**
* **Rubric:** **Entrance with the Censer** is mandatory for Great Vespers.
* **Hymn:** "O Gladsome Light" (Svite Tyhiy).


* **The Prokeimenon Slot:**
* **Resolve Case A (Saturday Evening):** "The Lord is King" (Tone 6).
* **Resolve Case B (Feast Day):** Prokeimenon of the Day (e.g., "Who is so great a God").
* **Resolve Case C (Lenten Sunday):** Great Prokeimenon ("Turn not away Thy face").


* **The Readings (Paremias) Slot (Part 3):**
* **Logic:** If Rank 3 (Polyeleos) or higher.
* **Action:** Insert **Old Testament Readings** (usually 3).
* **Pointer:** `[REF: Menaion_Readings_Group_ID]`.



---

### **V. The Litya (Optional Module)**

* **Trigger (Part 1):** Rank 4 (Vigil) or Rank 5 (Great Feast).
* **Structure:**
* Procession to the Narthex.
* **Stichera of Litya:** From Menaion (Feast) or Temple (Patron).
* **Litany of Litya:** "O God, save Thy people..."
* **Return:** "O Sovereign Lady..." (during return to nave).



---

### **VI. The Aposticha**

* **The Hymn Slot:**
* **Resolve Case A (Saturday Evening):** **Resurrectional Aposticha** (Octoechos).
* **Resolve Case B (Feast):** **Festal Aposticha** (Menaion).


* **The "Glory... Both Now..." Logic:**
* **Saturday:** Glory (Saint) -> Both Now (Theotokion of Saint's Tone **OR** Resurrectional if Saint has none).
* **Feast:** Glory (Feast) -> Both Now (Feast).



---

### **VII. The Troparia (The Conclusion)**

* **Song of Simeon:** "Now Thou lettest Thy servant depart..." (Read or Sung).
* **Trisagion Prayers:** Standard.
* **The Troparia Stack (Part 5 - Temple Rules):**
* **Scenario 1: Saturday Evening (Vigil):**
* "Rejoice, O Virgin Theotokos" (3 times) **IF** Litya was served.
* **OR:** Resurrectional Troparion -> Glory -> Saint -> Both Now -> Theotokion.


* **Scenario 2: Great Feast:**
* Troparion of the Feast (3 times).




* **The Artoklasia (Blessing of Loaves):**
* **Logic:** If Litya was served, the **Blessing of Bread, Wheat, Wine, and Oil** occurs here.



---

### **VIII. The Dismissal**

* **The Ending:**
* "Blessed be the Name of the Lord" (3 times).
* Psalm 33 (Read or Sung).


* **The Dismissal Proper:**
* **Type:** **Great Dismissal** ("May Christ our True God...").
* **Logic:** Mention the Resurrection (if Sunday), the Feast, the Temple Patron, and the Saint of the Day.
