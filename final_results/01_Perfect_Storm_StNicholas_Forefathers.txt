--- VESPERS (great_vespers_vigil) ---
[vesting_rite] 
   >>> RUBRIC: Vesting and Censing <<<
   (Source): Dolnytsky I:1
   [PRIEST]: Bless and vest in Epitrachelion. If Deacons serve, bless their Sticharia. Stand before the Holy Table.
   [DEACON]: Vest in Sticharion. Open the Holy Doors. Exclaim 'Bless, Master, the censer.' Perform the Great Censing of the Altar and the whole church.
   [SUBDEACON]: Light the candles on the Holy Table.

[opening_vigil] 
   >>> RUBRIC: The Opening <<<
   (Source): Dolnytsky I:2
   [DEACON]: Stand before the Holy Doors. Exclaim 'Bless, Master!'
   [PRIEST]: Make the sign of the Cross with the censer before the Holy Table. Sing 'Glory to the Holy, Consubstantial...'

   [FIXED]: horologion.blessing_vigil
[psalm_103_vigil] 
   >>> RUBRIC: The Proemial Psalm <<<
   (Source): Dolnytsky I:2
   [PRIEST]: Exit Sanctuary via North Door. Stand before closed Holy Doors, head uncovered. Read the Prayers of Light silently.
   [DEACON]: Close Holy Doors. Stand before them with a candle providing light for the Priest.
   [CANTOR]: Sing 'Come let us worship' and Psalm 103.

   [FIXED]: psalm_103

   (Source): Dolnytsky I: Matins

[gospel_rite] 
   >>> RUBRIC: The Gospel <<<
   (Source): Dolnytsky I: Matins
   [DEACON]: Exclaim 'Let us pray to the Lord'.
   [PRIEST]: Read the Gospel from the Holy Doors (or carry to Tetrapod on Sundays).
   [PEOPLE]: Venerate the Gospel after the reading.

--- LITURGY (liturgy_chrysostom) ---
[prothesis] 
   >>> RUBRIC: The Prothesis (Preparation) <<<
   (Source): Dolnytsky IV:385
   [PRIEST]: Prepare the Lamb. On Sundays and Feasts, cut the Lamb for the communicants. On Holy Thursday, prepare two Lambs (one for Presanctified).
   [DEACON]: Pour wine and water into the Chalice. Cover the Gifts.

[opening_blessing] 
   >>> RUBRIC: The Opening <<<
   [DEACON]: Exclaim 'Bless, Master!'
   [PRIEST]: Lift the Gospel Book. Make the sign of the Cross over the Antimension. Exclaim 'Blessed is the Kingdom...'

   [FIXED]: liturgikon.blessing_kingdom
[great_litany]    [FIXED]: horologion.litany_great
[antiphons_block] 
   >>> RUBRIC: The Antiphons <<<
   (Source): Dolnytsky II:102
   [CHOIR]: Sing the First Antiphon (or Typical Psalms).
   [DEACON]: Stand at the usual place. Exclaim the Small Litany between Antiphons.

[little_entrance] 
   >>> RUBRIC: The Little Entrance <<<
   [PRIEST]: Recite the Prayer of the Entrance silently. Bless the Entrance.
   [DEACON]: Carry the Gospel Book. Exclaim 'Wisdom, Upright!'
   [SUBDEACON]: Precede the Gospel with candles.

[troparia_kontakia_block] 
   >>> RUBRIC: Troparia and Kontakia <<<

[trisagion_module] 
   >>> RUBRIC: The Trisagion <<<

[readings_block] 
   >>> RUBRIC: The Readings <<<
   [READER]: Intone the Prokeimenon. Read the Epistle.
   [DEACON]: Cense the Altar and People during the Alleluia. Read the Gospel from the Ambo.
   [PRIEST]: Sit during the Epistle. Stand for the Gospel.

   [DEACON]: Cense the Altar, Icons, and People (Psalm 50).

   >>> RUBRIC: The Great Entrance <<<
   [DEACON]: Carry the Diskos (on head or in hands). Exclaim commemorations.
   [PRIEST]: Carry the Chalice. Exclaim commemorations.
   [SUBDEACON]: Hold the Aer and censer.
